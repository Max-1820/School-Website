    Annual Day Celebrations

    Was performed on friday 10th and saturday 11th in jan 2020

    DPS Bangalore South secures the RUNNERS UP position as INDIA'S MOST TALENTED SCHOOL in the Inter - School competition -SUNFEAST YIPEE WIZKIDS 2020 organized by KRAYON WIZKIDS. Our students competed against the best talent in the country and won many prizes. The competition was truly unique and was held virtually via the KRAYON APP!! Congratulations to the winners and the organizers who proved that nothing can deter you if there is a will to make it happen!!

    the cafeteria

    was built on 1996

    The school cafeteria is the most popular area on the school campus. Every care is taken to maintain hygiene and cleanliness and on the menu is a variety of wholesome dishes and healthy snacks. Vending machines for beverages and health drinks are also installed and the importance of healthy dietary habit is stressed.

    health clinic

    was built on 1998

    The well equipped health clinic provides medical attention to any student when the need arises. A qualified doctor assisted by two trained nurses is on duty throughout school hours on all working days providing the healing touch. A state – of -A the art ambulance is always on standby for any medical emergency that may arise. Two well qualified counsellors ensure the mental well being of our students.

    MATHLETICS
    was the 1st MATHLETICS

    “Mathematics is the most wonderful and powerful creation of the human spirit”. To assess the mathematical skills and aptitude of the students, the Junior Block of DPS, Bangalore South organized an Inter house Math quiz, “Mathletics” for the students of Grade 3, 4 and 5. Children geared up to face the challenges and fought it out through the various rounds like Mental maths, solving problems, arranging tangrams and rapid fire. Overall, it was an enriching and thrilling activity.

    72nd Independence Day celebrations
    Patriotic fervor marked the 72nd Independence Day at DPS Bangalore South.

    The proceedings commenced with the customary Flag hoisting ceremony. The Senior NCC Cadet commanded one and all present to salute the TIRANGA.. The National Anthem followed and so did the chanting ‘BHARAT MATA KI JAI’. The Principal Mrs. Manju Sharma also administered the pledge and emphasized upon the need to work towards nation building. The Cultural programme showcased the true meaning of patriotism by celebrating the diversity of our great country. The Inter house dance competition was a vibrant display of folk dances and were truly a spectacle for members of the audience. Dances and songs presented by the Primary wing accentuated the feeling of oneness… The programme concluded with the NCC Anthem ‘Hum Sab Bharatiya Hai’..
